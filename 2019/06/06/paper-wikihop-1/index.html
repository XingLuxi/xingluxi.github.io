Recent Advances on Multi-Hop RC - WikiHop

Posted on 2019-06-06 | Post modified: 2019-06-06 | In MRC-Paper-Intro | Post Total Visits
Words count in article: 93 | Reading time ≈ 1

WikiHop in QAngaroo benchmark is a dataset for Multi-hop Reading Comprehension Across Documents. Style: Multiple-Choice

Reference papers on WikiHop task:

  1. Question Answering by Reasoning Across Documents with Graph Convolutional Networks. NAACL,2019.
  2. Multi-hop Reading Comprehension across Multiple Documents by Reasoning over Heterogeneous Graphs. 2019.
  3. Coarse-Grain Fine-Grain Coattention Network for Multi-Evidence Question Answering. ICLR,2019.
  4. Neural models for reasoning over multiple mentions using coreference. NAACL,2018.
  5. Exploiting explicit paths for multihop reading comprehension. 2018.
  6. Exploring graph-structured passage representation for multihop reading comprehension with graph neural networks. 2018.
